Quantum Dot Measurement and Stability#

This section discusses the measurement of stable configurations of charges within quantum dot devices, like the ones that are being used at HRL today.

Summary#

In this section we cover

  • a basic understanding of quantum dots

  • how we can model the movement of charge carriers into and out of these dots

  • how we can use quantum dots to measure changes in the potential and correlate changes to the population of nearby dots

  • how quantum dots will interact with one another when they are coupled in series

  • how the properties of quantum dots in series can be systematically classified with stability diagrams

  • how all of these principles can help us understand the structure and function in HRL’s current quantum dot devices
